30MFF Movie

  • About

    This is a short film that is shot for the class “Communication Lab” at New York University Abu Dhabi, Summer 2021. This movie is shot within 15 minutes and is edited within 15 minutes.

    Watching the video above, you cannot move forward or backward to see the video. Instead, you have to watch the video without any control except the play/pause control. This shows the restrictions we all face during the pandemic.

    The film is about the precious memories that we all made in our freshman year. Since freshmen are transiting to the second year of college, this is the time where they stop and reflect about what happened in their first year at NYUAD.
